Welcome to Project Mind Capsule

Mind Capsule is an AI-powered personal companion designed to enhance daily life by providing personalized support, memory assistance, and emotional connection. Utilizing advanced natural language processing and local data storage for maximum privacy, Mind Capsule aims to become a trusted, accessible, and user-friendly tool for individuals seeking a supportive AI companion. We are seeking NJEDA grant funding to finalize development, conduct user testing, and launch Mind Capsule as a leading AI companion solution.

  1. Company Description:

    Mind Capsule is dedicated to developing and deploying AI companion technology that prioritizes user privacy and emotional well-being. Our initial product focuses on a "Companion Version" that establishes a foundation for future expansion into specialized memory support and medical applications. We are committed to creating an intuitive and accessible user experience, ensuring that Mind Capsule can be easily integrated into daily life. We will focus on a subscription based business model, where users pay a monthly fee for the device, and support. 3. Products and Services:

    Mind Capsule Companion Version: Voice-activated AI companion that engages in natural conversations. Personalized memory prompts and reminders. Ability to store and retrieve important information. Optional visual display for photos, reminders, and visual cues. Local data storage for maximum privacy. Subscription based service, including device, and support.
